nothing tops cute thing as a song that makes me ill currently, but lets go back in my brain and find another song that truely dyes the thread of my being.

ooooh glass beach. i don't think theres a band that has the same place in my heart as glass beach. i was introduced to them during a very tranistional time in my life, and one day i went from projecting these songs, and this song a lot, from one person, to another. now this song is kinda inspiring to me. its about moving out and having your first place ( i swore i read this somewhere i can't find this ) and i guess both meanings of this song for me, were about changing, about growing.
the sound of glass beach and i would argue 5th wave emo is, nostalgia, and sickness. this song sounds so sweet and nostaglic in a way i can't describe in just how it sounds. the intro with the soft vocals and instruments, sounidng almost etheral, progressing into a catchy hook, with lyrics reminissing of past mistakes, knocking holes into walls, trying to make a place feel like a home. it, its sickening, and i can't stop listening. the juxtaposition of the ethereal sound and the depressing lycics, yet both being nostalgic just creates a track that makes me dream about the better times. anything to bring me close to you, friday nights, foggy streets and christmas lights.
but things are better now, im better now, im diffrent now, im almost quite litterly a diffrent person. and i was diffrent when this song had its first meaning to me, so why did i want her to take my hand then, and why am i left paralyzed even though i finally got a decent job? is it because you won't always be my friend? i guess i just gotta hold out for brighter better days.
